How to Save, Rate, Comment and Reply to a Question

Made by Chang Chang with Scribe



1. Navigate to https://questify-frontend.onrender.com/questions

2. Select a question

3. Click "Save" to save the question

4. Click the stars to rate the question.

5. Click "Confirm" to subbmit the rating.

6. Click the "Leave your own feedback field to post comment.

7. Click "Submit" to subbmit comment

8. Click "Reply" under a comment to reply to it.

9. Click the "Write a reply..." field to write the reply.

10. Click "Post Reply" to post the reply.
