How to Filter, Sort and Search Questions

Made by Chang Chang with Scribe



1. Navigate to https://questify-frontend.onrender.com/questions

2. Click "Filter"

3. Select the filter options.

4. Click "Apply filters"

5. Click the x to clear filter.

6. Click this button to show sort options

7. Select an sorting option

8. Click the "Search questions" field.

9. Type your search keyword (can search by title / week / topic)

10. Click "Search"

11. Click the x to clear search.
