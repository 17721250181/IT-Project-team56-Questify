Create a New Account on Questify

Made by Chang Chang with Scribe



1. Navigate to https://questify-frontend.onrender.com/login

2. Click "Create New Account"

3. Enter your display name

4. Enter your student ID

5. Enter your student email address

6. Enter password

7. Click the "Confirm your password" field.

8. Click "I agree to the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy"

9. Click "Create Account"
