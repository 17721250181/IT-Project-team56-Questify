How to Post a Short Answer Question on Questify

Made by Chang Chang with Scribe



1. Navigate to https://questify-frontend.onrender.com/

2. Click "Post Question"

3. Select an option from "Week & Topic".

4. Enter your question in the "Question" field.

5. Enter the answer in the "Expected answer" field.

6. Click "Create Question"
