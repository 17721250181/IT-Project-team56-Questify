Team Decision Making

  • Major decisions are finalised during in-person meetings and documented in meeting notes.

    • The initial idea was developed after receiving substantial feedback from last year’s student survey (https://docs.google.com/forms/d/177IG2Yw7_hmkV3A-LJI6tIBEWOP35m23V-1EhOGZTlg/edit#question=640965715&field=1056663520)

    • The idea was then refined through several in-person meetings where all members contributed, along with feedback from both the client and tutors. These discussions are also documented in all the Team Meeting notes aforementioned. Ex. https://chma-unimelb-student.atlassian.net/wiki/x/F4B-/

    • Following this, the team prepared a proposal and confirmed the direction in a Zoom meeting with the client. (https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1o851PettboyNslZbZrJeO9dDQ9HtaW_fN1rCGGsCiaU/edit?usp=sharing )

    • Details of some certain features has been further discussed and then improved during in-person meeting or improved based on client/tutor feedback.

  • Minor decisions (e.g., scheduling, small task allocation) are resolved via quick discussions on Instagram.

  • The team generally follows a consensus-based approach, ensuring all members contribute to decision-making.
