Create a Multiple Choice Question on Questify

Made by Chang Chang with Scribe



1. Navigate to https://questify-frontend.onrender.com/questions

2. Click "Post Question"

3. Click "Multiple Choice"

4. Select an option from "Week & Topic".

5. Enter your question in the "Question" field.

6. Enter the options.

7. Select the correct options

8. Click "Create Question"
